Created for All

DUC and DUCX were designed to be open and inclusive. Tech savvy or not, we believe that everyone should have the ability to use reliable payment services that are as simple and common as using a credit card.

Our Mission

  • To implement a fast blockchain with low fees.
  • To always present a clear and easy to navigate interface.
  • To be friendly enough to allow any user to control, support or make decisions and take part in the development and life of a project.

The Ducatus Wallet

The Ducatus ecosystem exists as a web-wallet that runs on all desktop platforms Windows, Linux, macOS and mobile for iOS platforms and Android. Users can send transactions and accept payments not only in DUC and DUCX but also in Bitcoin, Bitcoin Cash, XRP, Ethereum and USDC, PAX and GUSD tokens. More coins and tokens will be added in the future.

frequently asked questions

What is the difference between DUC and DUCX?
While DUC and DUCX are complementary in the Ducatus ecosystem, each has its own characteristics and functionalities:

• DUC is based on Litecoin, DUCX is based on Ethereum.
• DUCX supports smart contracts.
• DUC is used primarily as a mode of payment within the Ducatus cashless economy.
• DUCX is used primarily for trading.

How do I buy DUC or DUCX?
You can buy DUC online at https://www.ducatuscoins.com/buy . To acquire DUCX for the first time, you can buy DUC and then swap it for DUCX in the Ducatus Wallet. 1 DUCX = 10 DUC.

How do I exchange DUC or DUCX?
As DUC is created to be a payment coin, it is not meant to be exchanged or traded directly in a cryptocurrency exchange. It may be swapped for DUCX inside the Ducatus Wallet. DUCX may then be traded at PancakeSwap .

Where can I spend DUC or DUCX?
DUCX functions as a trading token and is not currently used for merchant payments.
DUC, on the other hand, can be used as a mode of payment (like fiat). With more merchant partnerships in the works and continuous merchant recruitment, more people will be able to use DUC in everyday life.

• Use DUC to pay for goods and services from merchants that accept DUC as payment.
• Use DUC to lease parts of projects at the Centurion Lease & Earn platform.
• Use DUC to pay for pre-loved items at Remusnation D-Bay.
• Give DUC as gifts. Some forward-thinking users have given it away like a cash gift voucher.
